# 📊 Financial Modeling & Valuation Analysis – Escorts Kubota Ltd.

## 🎯 Project Objective

Developed a comprehensive financial model for Escorts Kubota Ltd. to analyze historical performance, forecast future financials, estimate intrinsic value, and evaluate key business drivers.

The model integrates forecasting, ratio analysis, WACC estimation, DCF valuation, variance analysis, sensitivity testing, and interactive dashboards to support investment research and financial decision-making.

## 🔎 Key Insights

- Revenue growth has moderated in recent years, reflecting business maturity.
- EBITDA margins expanded significantly over the last decade through operational efficiency improvements.
- EPS demonstrated strong long-term growth despite cyclical fluctuations.
- Interest coverage improved substantially, indicating stronger debt-servicing capacity.
- Cash flow quality strengthened as CFO/Sales improved over time.
- Valuation remains highly sensitive to assumptions regarding growth and discount rates.
